About Me

I am a driven and eager student at the School of Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University. As an aspiring app developer and web designer, I am well-versed in both programming and design, including the languages Java, HTML and CSS, Python and Processing, and the design software Adobe Suite and Figma. My work focuses on creating an enjoyable, functional design for users who interact with my work. As a designer, I use a minimalistic style to create breathable and light designs that enable an efficient and enjoyable user experience.
